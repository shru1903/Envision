Our Vision

Our vision is to create a better tomorrow through the means of beneficial service. Making the world a better place is in humanity's hands and in our heads...

What we offer:

Do Tasks, Earn Stuff!


Doing the tasks gives you coins. The tasks like plant a sapling and collect plastics from your area rewards you with coins. These coins can be redeemed for ecological goodies and more! The tasks are simple yet effective and would help the environment in many ways. Surprise gifts are dropped in usually, so gather coins!

DIY It!


DIY products made of recyclable and reusable materials are one of the best things you should make to stay creative, imaginative and also help the environment. We give you ideas to create things out of waste and you can add your piece of idea too! Doing these rewards you with Coins (Greecos)

Exchange Books


You need a book...Come across and go through the book listings. You might get the book you need in exchange for one you have. Send it to the post code given, and Voila! You receive the book you need, the other person gets the book they need and a few trees save themselves from getting cut (Terms and Conditions apply) You can also post your books here for others to see and exchange (for free, of course!)

Material' Collection


One man's waste is not only another man's treasure but the environment's trasure. There are many a few organizations and factories who collect recycable and dry waste. We act as a middle-body to make sure that the materials don't go to waste or to the delicate soil.
